Armstrong Maple Leaf II

Click to view:Larger

One of the advantages of living where the leaves make dramatic color changes is watching them do just that. Add some hurricane gale force winds and the right combination of trees can produce a dazzling shower of confetti. A sudden updraft, as the sun winks at the clouds, and it turns into a daytime fireworks display. Impossible to paint that on any day regardless of the medium.
